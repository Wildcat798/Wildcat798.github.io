Who am I?

Hi! Nice to meet you! I'm your friendly neighborhood Full-Stack Software Developer! Guessing you want my origin story...I know, every awesome Full-Stack superhero has one! I'm originally from Atlanta, Georgia. I started my first job at 14 years old and I've been working ever since. So, it only made sense for me to join the military and apply that loyalty, dedication, and work ethic towards taking an oath for something bigger than myself! Fast forward several years, an awesome marriage, and 2 amazing little kids later, I'm a disabled veteran looking for something else that I feel would challenge me, as well as being able to showcase a product that I would be proud of!

Intro my love for software developing and the start of my journey to becoming a Full-Stack Software Developer!
